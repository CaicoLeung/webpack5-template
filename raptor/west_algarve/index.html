Out & About in West Algarve

This former fishing village has evolved into a very family friendly hotspot of the Western Algarve, offering a wide range of properties, from flats to luxurious cliff top villas. The village nestles overlooking its pretty bay and the famous Black Rock. Excellent supermarkets, great restaurants, welcoming street cafes and bars give this village something for all. The Chestertons office is situated in a prime position in the centre of the village.
